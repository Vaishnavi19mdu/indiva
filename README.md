# INDIVA

A premium social impact platform focused on elderly companionship, wellness, and community care.

Built for the Next.js UI/UX Hackathon 2026.

---

## Overview

INDIVA is a modern nonprofit experience designed to address one of the most overlooked social issues — elderly loneliness and emotional isolation.

Rather than functioning as a traditional donation website, INDIVA was designed as an emotionally immersive digital experience that combines storytelling, interaction design, and thoughtful user experience to create meaningful engagement.

The platform focuses on:

* companionship support
* wellness assistance
* emotional connection
* volunteer participation
* transparent community impact

The overall design philosophy blends:

* Apple-inspired minimalism
* Airbnb warmth
* editorial storytelling
* calm healthcare aesthetics

---

# Core Idea

The project explores how emotional design can improve trust, participation, and empathy in nonprofit platforms.

Instead of presenting users with static donation forms, INDIVA guides them through:

* human stories
* emotional interactions
* community participation
* visual impact experiences

The goal was to make support feel personal rather than transactional.

# Key Features

## Multi-Page Experience

INDIVA is structured as a realistic multi-page platform instead of a single scrolling landing page.

* Community

## Emotional Storytelling

The platform uses cinematic storytelling and editorial layouts to create emotional connection through:

* immersive content sections
* warm imagery
* human-centered copywriting
* calm visual pacing

---

## Interactive Community Features

### A Day With Them

An interactive storytelling experience showing a senior’s daily life before and after receiving support.

### Letters Across Generations

A floating message experience featuring emotional exchanges between:

* seniors
* volunteers
* children

### Send Warmth

Users can submit supportive messages that animate into a live emotional message wall.

### Impact Ripple

Interactive ripple visualization showing how donations spread through communities.

### Memory Garden

Every donation symbolically grows a shared digital garden through glowing flowers, lanterns, and light particles.

---

# Mobile-First Experience

INDIVA was designed mobile-first to ensure:

* spacious layouts
* clean navigation
* large touch targets
* uncluttered interfaces
* smooth responsive behavior

The mobile experience avoids:

* harsh divider lines
* cramped sections
* excessive borders
* overcomplicated navigation patterns

A premium slide-over drawer navigation system was used for mobile devices.

---

# Volunteer Experience

The platform includes a realistic volunteer onboarding flow featuring:

* multi-step interactions
* personal information collection
* interests & skills selection
* availability scheduling
* optional identity verification upload

The experience was intentionally designed to feel welcoming rather than bureaucratic.
